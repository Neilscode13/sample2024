About Me

Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Neil and I'm excited to share a little bit about myself with you.

In 2018, I graduated from George Brown College with a diploma in Business Marketing. During my time there, I learned the skills necessary to create impactful marketing campaigns and hone my critical thinking abilities. While I found the field of marketing fascinating, I felt a pull towards the world of web development.

Since then, I've been teaching myself web development, and it's been an incredibly rewarding journey. I love the challenge of creating clean, efficient code and bringing my creative ideas to life through website design. Learning web development has also taught me valuable problem-solving skills and has allowed me to create my own projects from scratch.

Lastly, I'm a lifelong learner at heart. I believe in constantly expanding my knowledge and skill set, whether it's through online courses, tutorials, or books. I'm always eager to learn new programming languages, frameworks, and techniques that can improve my work and help me grow as a developer.

On this website, you will find a collection of my work, ideas, and musings on a variety of topics. I hope to inspire you to explore your own creativity and to never stop learning. Thank you for visiting!
